Hey, I'm Selma

I've been writing since I could hold a pen okay, maybe not *that* early, but you get the idea. I mix my love for words with my obsession with tech, and now I’m knee-deep in AI and machine learning. You’ll usually find me diving into computer vision (yes, I’m doing my Master’s in it right now, first year—*no pressure, right?*).

In between trying to figure out why my code doesn't work (again), I’m writing about everything from AI projects to tech life, with a healthy dose of sarcasm to keep things interesting. I might even throw in a few reflections on life, tech, and whatever else is bugging me at the moment.

On this blog, I’m sharing my journey my successes, my failures, and all the weird in-betweens that come with learning and building cool stuff. So buckle up, because it’s a mix of technical deep dives and, yes, maybe a little bit of my sarcastic humor thrown in for good measure. Enjoy the ride!
